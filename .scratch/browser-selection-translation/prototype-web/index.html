Field note

The quiet work of keeping attention intact

A good reading tool should arrive only when it is needed. It should clarify the unfamiliar word in front of you, then get out of the way before the sentence loses its shape.

The hardest part is not translation quality alone. It is preserving the rhythm of reading while still making a useful memory trace for later review.

Salto treats this moment as a small interruption with long-term value: selection, translation, optional save, and back to the page.
